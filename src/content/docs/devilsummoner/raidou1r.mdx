## Speedrun History
<Tabs>
    <TabItem label="Gumshoe">
        1. RAIDOU Remastered - The Mystery of the Soulless Army was released on July 19th, 2025.
        2. Symm completed the first speedrun on Gumshoe difficulty on 2025/06/23 with a time of 4:11:00. He later improved it to a [3:56:18](https://youtu.be/o-7ZgdCFK_k) using a Spear-based route.
        3. The fastest time for Gumshoe difficulty is [3:45:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BEShyHm_p6s) achieved by ToxicTT on 2025/07/12, using the axe route introduced in Private Eye runs by mizusima40.
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="Private Eye">
        1. RAIDOU Remastered - The Mystery of the Soulless Army was released on July 19th, 2025.
        2. mizusima40 completed the first speedrun on Private Eye difficulty on 2025/07/05 with a time of 4:11:04.
        3. The current world record is held by mizusima40 with a run done on 2025/07/11 and a time of [**3:50:31**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gjTSOr7zn5Q).
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>
