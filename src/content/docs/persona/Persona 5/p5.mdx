## Speedrun History
<Steps>
    1. Persona 5 was released in Japan on September 15, 2016.
    2. The first recorded run was done by Neviutz with a time of [**22:52:47**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/133249423) which he'd later improve to a [**18:14:32**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/144789820)
    3. On 2018/03/29, Liv would set a new world record with a time of [**18:07:22**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/244378107)
    4. 2 days later, Neviutz reclaimed the world record with a time of [**17:05:12**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/245162770). 9 months later, he'd improve the time to [**16:15:08**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/354822567)
    5. On 2019/07/15, Darkkefka set a new world record with a time of [**16:12:32**](https://youtu.be/5KKGyj_lwN0)
    6. On 2020/05/28, Shady claimed the world record with a time of [**16:00:20**](https://youtu.be/9_pOjRyeyW8), keeping it and later improving it to a time of [**15:08:48**](https://youtu.be/paJwHqTnmik)
    7. On 2021/04/224, Darkkefka reclaimed the world record with a time of [**15:06:15**](https://youtu.be/bIu_f8SbDS0)
    8. The current world record holder is Sprayz with a time of [**15:04:32**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2122423767)
</Steps>
