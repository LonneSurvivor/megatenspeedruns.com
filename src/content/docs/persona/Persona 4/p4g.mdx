## Speedrun History
<Tabs syncKey='persona4'>
    <TabItem label="Hard, Golden Ending - Legacy">
        <Steps>
            1. Persona 4: Golden was released in Japan on June 14, 2012.
            2. The first recorded run was done by mulsqi with a time of [**11:36:08**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/4252044)
            3. The current world record holder is mulsqi with a time of [**8:43:25**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/537323140)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="Hard, Golden Ending - Remaster">
        <Steps>
            1. Persona 4: Golden was remastered and released worldwide on January 19, 2023.
            2. The current world record holder is asapska with a time of [**8:25:59**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1732454331)
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
    <TabItem label="Hard, Golden Ending - PC">
        <Steps>
            1. Persona 4: Golden was remastered and released worldwide on June 13, 2020.
            2. The first recorded run was done by mulsqi with a time of [**6:21:44**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/767379402)
            3. On 2023/07/21, Radu set a new world record with a time of [**6:19:39**](https://youtu.be/kIg3IAYJtMQ)
            4. On 2024/05/14, mulsqi reclaimed the world record and achieved the first sub 6h run with a time of [**5:56:33**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2148997777)
            5. On 2024/07/24, mulsqi improved the world record with a time of [**5:52:15**](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2236876035)
            6. For the first time in over a year, the world record holder changed when on 2025/02/10 Kiitoksia established a new record of [**5:49:54**](https://youtu.be/DqZG-Nab8ns).
        </Steps>
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>
